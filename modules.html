How NIM Works

Get your school fully digital in 5 simple steps. No technical expertise required.

Step 01

Add Students & Classes

Start by setting up your school profile, adding classes, sections, and onboarding students with complete profiles and parent information.

Step 02

Manage Academics & Attendance

Assign subjects, teachers, and timetables. Track daily student and staff attendance with automatic absence alerts for parents.

Step 03

Conduct Exams & Generate Results

Plan exams, enter marks, and let NIM automatically calculate results and generate professional report cards for every student.

Step 04

Collect Fees & Track Finance

Set fee structures, collect payments, issue receipts, track expenses, and manage your institution's complete financial health in real-time.

Step 05

View Reports & Insights

Access powerful dashboards with student performance, attendance trends, financial summaries, and institutional statistics — anytime, anywhere.
